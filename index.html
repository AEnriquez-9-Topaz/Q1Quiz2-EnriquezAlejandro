Spider-man: Into the Spider-verse




Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse is a movie made in 2018 that has gone on to be one of the most critically-aclaimed animated movies of all times, winning numerous awards such as the BET . This movie showcases the story of a black, teenager boy, who suffers with teenager problems such as peer pressure, school, family, adnd even puberty. However, his biggest problem, are the expectations set for him . As an academic achiever, society places much pressure for him. In this story, he accidently gets bit by a radioactive spider, becoming Spider-Man at the time the old Spider-Man just died , in an event that caused a multiversal fracture, putting other Spider-Mans into his universe . With the multiverse at stake, this young teen has to go against his own self-doubt, dangerous villains, and the reality of suddenly being put in a superhero situation and having to handle all of it as once. Will he suceed with the help of his new Spider-Man friends. Or will he crackdown as the world does so with him?




Miles Morales

The main character of the story. He gets bit by a radioactive spider and is given the job of Spiderman right as the multiverse is about to break


Gwen Stacy

The partner of Miles. She is someone who got transported to Miles' universe and is guiding him as he learns to be Spiderman.


Dad of miles, Jefferson

The dad of Miles. Acts as his supporter, yet he is also the person who gives so much pressure to Miles which is a main plot point.


Kingpin

Person trying to destroy the multiverse, while also being the boss of Miles' uncle. Main villain for the Spiderman.
